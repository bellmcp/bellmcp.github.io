Where do we go when we fall asleep? The evil little twin represented a nightmare surrounded by the darkness and creepy things. Inspired by Stanley Kubrick's The Shining (1980).


“These images are from my dreams. I don't know why, but I think of these patterns and places and monsters, especially this one, as my conscience. I feel like I'm a monster inside. It's like I'm the monster under my own bed.”


“When I have dreams, I just draw everything. I write down everything that my dreams remind me of. I could dream these things and just keep them in my head, and then nothing would ever happen with them, and then none of this would be here.”

— Billie Ellish
